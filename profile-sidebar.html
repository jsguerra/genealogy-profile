Biography

John William Carter was born on 12 March 1842 in Richmond, Virginia to Thomas Abram Carter and Mary Elizabeth Holden. He grew up on the family farm in Henrico County and attended the local schoolhouse before taking over agricultural duties in his late teens.

In 1866 he married Eleanor Frances Monroe in Albemarle County. The couple settled near Charlottesville, where John combined farming with public service, serving as County Clerk from 1878 to 1895. He was known in the community for his meticulous record-keeping and his involvement in the local Methodist church.

John passed away on 7 November 1918 at his home in Charlottesville. He and Eleanor are buried side by side at Riverview Cemetery.
