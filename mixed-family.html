Biography

Margaret Anne Sullivan was born on 3 July 1938 in Albany, New York to James Patrick Sullivan and Dorothy Mae Kessler. Her parents divorced in 1944, and her mother remarried Harold Eugene Beck in 1946. Margaret was raised in a blended household alongside Harold's son from his first marriage, growing up in Schenectady, New York.

She married Richard Allen Howe in 1960. The couple had two children before Richard's untimely passing in 1968. In 1971, Margaret married Dr. George Franklin Price in Chatham County, Georgia. Together they had one daughter and adopted a son through Catholic Charities in 1975. George also brought two children from his previous marriage into the family.

Margaret spent over thirty years as a school teacher and later a librarian in the Savannah–Chatham County school system. She was active in her parish, volunteered with local literacy programs, and was a devoted genealogist who traced her Sullivan line back to County Cork, Ireland. She passed away peacefully on 19 January 2021 and is buried at Bonaventure Cemetery.
